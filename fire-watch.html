Fire Watch Services

Certified fire watch personnel deployed whenever your sprinkler or suppression systems are impaired — keeping you fully compliant with California fire code and OSHA requirements.

Service Overview

What Is Fire Watch?

Fire watch is a legally required service when a building's fire suppression system — sprinklers, alarms, or fire suppression equipment — is taken offline for maintenance, repair, or due to an unexpected failure. California fire code and OSHA regulations mandate that a trained individual physically monitor the affected area around the clock until the system is back in service.

Invincible Inc's fire watch officers are trained specifically for this role. They understand California fire code requirements, know how to recognize and respond to fire hazards, maintain detailed hourly logs, and know exactly when and how to contact the fire department. Our rapid deployment capability means we can have certified personnel on-site within hours of your call — day or night.

Failing to maintain a proper fire watch during a system impairment can result in significant fines, permit suspensions, and liability exposure. More importantly, an untrained or inattentive fire watch can fail to detect a developing fire before it becomes catastrophic. Invincible Inc eliminates both risks.

Important: California Health & Safety Code and local fire codes typically require fire watch to begin within 15 minutes of a system impairment. Do not delay — call us immediately when your system goes offline.

What You Get

Key Features

Our fire watch service is purpose-built for code compliance, rapid response, and thorough documentation.

24/7 Continuous Monitoring

Fire watch officers are deployed for the full duration of any system impairment — days, nights, weekends, and holidays. There are no gaps in coverage until your system is restored and verified.

California Fire Code Compliance

Our officers understand NFPA 25, California Health & Safety Code, and local AHJ (Authority Having Jurisdiction) requirements — ensuring your fire watch operation meets every regulatory standard.

Hourly Patrol Logs

Officers complete and timestamp patrol logs every hour, documenting conditions observed throughout the monitored area. These logs are provided to the client and available for fire marshal inspection.

Immediate Fire Emergency Response

In the event of a fire or smoke detection, officers immediately initiate evacuation procedures, call 9-1-1, and alert building management — fulfilling their core protective function.

Fire Department Coordination

Our officers know the correct notification procedures and work cooperatively with the local fire department. Many jurisdictions require fire department notification when a system is impaired — we handle this.

Hot Work & Construction Monitoring

For construction sites and renovation projects involving welding, cutting, or grinding, our officers monitor for sparks and ignition sources both during and after work periods per NFPA guidelines.
