Unarmed Security Services

Professional, uniformed security officers providing a visible deterrent, access control, and responsive incident management — without the use of firearms.

Service Overview

What Is Unarmed Security?

Unarmed security services provide a professional, uniformed presence that deters criminal activity, enforces site policies, and ensures the safety of your patrons, employees, and assets. Invincible Inc's unarmed officers are trained to observe, report, and respond — and to de-escalate situations before they become incidents.

Our officers undergo California BSIS guard card certification as well as Invincible Inc's own comprehensive training program. They arrive in professional uniforms, carry incident report materials, and operate according to your facility's specific post orders. Whether you need a single overnight post or a team managing a multi-entrance facility, we scale to your needs.

Unarmed security is the right choice for environments where a visible, professional deterrent is effective and where customer interaction and approachability are valued. It is our most flexible and widely deployed service.

Best for: Retail centers, apartment communities, office parks, warehouses, schools, events, and any location where visible deterrence and access management matter most.

What You Get

Key Features

Our unarmed security service is built around six core capabilities that protect your people and property.

Uniformed Officer Presence

Professionally dressed officers in Invincible Inc uniforms provide an immediate visible deterrent to theft, vandalism, and unauthorized access.

Access Control

Officers manage entry and exit points, verify credentials, issue visitor badges, and enforce access policies to keep unauthorized individuals out.

CCTV Monitoring

Where systems are available, officers actively monitor security camera feeds and document any unusual or suspicious activity in real time.

Incident Reporting

Every incident is thoroughly documented in written reports that are submitted to management at the end of each shift — creating a clear record and chain of accountability.

Patrol & Perimeter Checks

Officers conduct scheduled and random patrol routes throughout your facility, checking doors, gates, and perimeter fencing to identify and deter unauthorized entry.

Customer & Visitor Assistance

Our officers are trained in professional communication and customer service — directing visitors, answering questions, and representing your brand positively.
