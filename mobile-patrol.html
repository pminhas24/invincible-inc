Mobile Patrol Services

GPS-tracked marked patrol vehicles providing a highly visible security presence across your entire property — a cost-effective alternative to stationary posts.

Service Overview

What Is Mobile Patrol?

Mobile patrol security deploys marked security vehicles and uniformed officers on scheduled and randomized patrol routes throughout your facility, parking areas, and perimeter. The combination of a visible marked vehicle and a professional security officer is one of the most powerful deterrents available.

Unlike stationary posts, mobile patrol covers more ground at a fraction of the cost — making it ideal for large properties, multi-building campuses, and facilities that don't need round-the-clock foot coverage but still require regular, professional security presence. Our patrol officers check all access points, document conditions, and respond to any alarm or suspicious activity immediately.

All patrol vehicles are GPS-tracked in real time, and officers submit timestamped electronic patrol logs at the completion of each check. This gives clients a transparent, verifiable record of every patrol conducted.

Best for: Construction sites, solar farms, warehouses, commercial parks, multi-unit residential, large retail centers, and any property too large for cost-effective stationary coverage.

What You Get

Key Features

Mobile patrol delivers professional security across your entire property — not just at a single post.

Marked Patrol Vehicles

Branded, clearly marked security vehicles make patrols unmistakably visible — maximizing deterrence to trespassers, vandals, and would-be thieves.

GPS-Tracked Routes

Every patrol unit is GPS-monitored in real time by our operations center. Route completion, check-in points, and response times are all verifiable and reportable.

Scheduled & Random Patrols

We combine scheduled patrol intervals with randomized check times — making it impossible for unauthorized individuals to predict gaps in coverage.

Alarm Response

When your alarm is triggered, a patrol officer is dispatched immediately to investigate, secure the property, and coordinate with law enforcement if needed.

Electronic Patrol Logs

Officers submit timestamped digital patrol reports at the end of every shift, giving you a complete, auditable record of all activity and conditions observed.

Lock / Unlock & Property Checks

Patrol officers check all doors, gates, windows, and access points during each sweep — securing any open entry and flagging any damage or vulnerability for immediate follow-up.
