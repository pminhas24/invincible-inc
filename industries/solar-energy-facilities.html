Solar Energy Facilities

Remote-site security for solar farms, substations, battery storage areas, and renewable infrastructure across California.

Industry Overview

Security challenges at solar and energy sites

Solar facilities often sit in remote locations with large open perimeters, limited staffing, and expensive exposed equipment. Copper theft, vandalism, trespassing, illegal dumping, and attempted tampering with fenced infrastructure can create major operational and repair costs.

Because these properties cover so much ground, security planning has to focus on perimeter discipline, gate control, patrol consistency, and fast reporting. Many sites also need officers who can coordinate with technicians, contractors, and utility stakeholders without interrupting maintenance work or access schedules.

Invincible Security Group provides BSIS licensed guards for California renewable energy sites with patrol programs built around remote risk. We help owners and operators improve deterrence, protect valuable equipment, and maintain better awareness across substations, arrays, roads, and entry points.

Best fit: Utility-scale solar farms, battery energy storage sites, substations, inverter yards, and related renewable energy infrastructure.
