About Invincible Inc

Built on Trust.
Proven by Results.

Invincible Inc was founded in Bakersfield, California with a single mission: to deliver professional security services that clients can genuinely rely on. Today, we serve businesses, communities, and facilities across California — from the Central Valley to Southern California and beyond.

Every officer on our team is thoroughly vetted, state-licensed through BSIS, and trained to handle real-world security challenges with professionalism and composure. We don't just fill posts — we protect people and property with integrity.
