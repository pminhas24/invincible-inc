What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive security solutions tailored to your industry and environment.

Unarmed Security

Professional unarmed security officers trained for access control, patrol, and incident response — providing a visible deterrent and professional presence.

Armed Security

Highly trained armed officers for high-risk environments. Our personnel hold all required state certifications and provide maximum deterrence when it matters most.

Mobile Patrol

Regular vehicle patrols across your property or multiple locations, providing visibility and rapid response with detailed patrol reports every shift.

Fire Watch

Certified fire watch services for construction sites, buildings with impaired systems, or hot work operations — keeping your site compliant and protected around the clock.

Concierge Security

Polished, professional security personnel who double as front-of-house ambassadors — combining elite access control with exceptional guest and tenant experience.
