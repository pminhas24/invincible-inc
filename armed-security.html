Armed Security Services

Licensed, trained armed security officers providing the highest level of deterrence and protection for high-risk environments and high-value assets.

Service Overview

What Is Armed Security?

Armed security services deploy licensed officers who carry firearms in accordance with California law. These officers provide the maximum level of deterrence for environments where the stakes are high — whether that means protecting cash assets, securing a high-risk facility, or safeguarding personnel in volatile situations.

Every armed officer at Invincible Inc holds both a California BSIS guard card and a valid firearms permit issued by the Bureau of Security and Investigative Services. They undergo extensive training in use-of-force law, safe firearm handling, threat assessment, de-escalation, and emergency response protocols before ever being deployed.

We understand that deploying armed security carries significant responsibility. Our rigorous vetting, background investigation, and training processes ensure that every armed officer represents the highest standard of professionalism, restraint, and competency.

Best for: Financial institutions, jewelry stores, cannabis dispensaries, high-value warehouses, executive protection, and any environment where maximum deterrence and rapid threat response are essential.

Key Features

Our armed security program is built on rigorous training, strict licensing, and professional discipline.

State-Licensed Firearm Carry

All armed officers carry current BSIS firearms permits and are qualified with their issued weapons on a regular schedule to maintain peak readiness.

Threat Assessment & De-escalation

Officers are trained to identify, assess, and de-escalate threats before force is ever considered — ensuring measured, appropriate, and legally defensible responses.

High-Value Asset Protection

Our armed officers are specifically trained for environments with cash handling, precious inventory, or sensitive materials requiring an elevated level of security posture.

Armed Mobile Patrol

Armed patrol officers in marked vehicles conduct regular property sweeps, providing a visible deterrent combined with rapid armed response capability.

Emergency Response Coordination

Armed officers are trained to coordinate with local law enforcement during critical incidents, serving as the first professional line of response while awaiting police arrival.

Detailed Use-of-Force Reporting

Every incident involving a response is thoroughly documented with detailed reports. Our officers maintain proper chain of evidence and communication with management and legal teams.
