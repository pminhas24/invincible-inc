Unarmed Security Guard Services

Professional, BSIS-licensed unarmed security officers providing visible deterrence and protection across California — 24/7.

What We Provide

Professional Unarmed Security Officers

Invincible Security Group's unarmed security officers provide a highly visible, professional security presence that deters crime before it happens. Our guards are BSIS licensed, thoroughly trained, and deployed with site-specific post orders tailored to your property's unique needs and risk profile.

Whether you need a single security officer for a commercial property or a full team covering multiple access points, our unarmed guards are equipped to handle access control, patrol, incident reporting, and emergency response. Every officer undergoes rigorous background checks, drug screening, and field training before their first shift.

We understand that a security guard is often the first point of contact for visitors, tenants, and employees. That's why our officers are trained not just in security protocols, but in professional conduct, communication, and customer service — ensuring every interaction reflects well on your organization while keeping your facility safe.
