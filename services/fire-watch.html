Fire Watch Security Services

NFPA and local fire code compliant fire watch officers — deployed rapidly for sprinkler impairments, construction, and hot work operations.

Code Compliance

Fire Watch Officers — Rapid Deployment

When a fire suppression system is impaired, under maintenance, or temporarily offline, local fire codes and NFPA 25 standards require continuous fire watch coverage. Invincible Security Group provides trained fire watch officers who can be deployed rapidly — often within hours of your call — to ensure your property remains in compliance and your occupants remain safe.

Our fire watch officers are trained to conduct continuous rounds of the affected areas, identify fire hazards, maintain detailed watch logs, and respond immediately in the event of a fire by notifying occupants and contacting emergency services. They operate independently of your existing staff and carry no operational burden on your facility management team.

Fire watch is also required during hot work operations such as welding, cutting, or grinding — when sparks or heat can ignite surrounding materials. ISG can provide dedicated fire watch personnel to monitor hot work activities and ensure immediate response capability for the required hold-time period following work completion.
