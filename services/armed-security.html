Maximum Protection

Armed Security for High-Risk Environments

Invincible Security Group's armed security officers are among the most thoroughly vetted and trained security professionals in California. Each armed officer holds a valid California BSIS Security Guard Registration and an active BSIS Firearm Permit — meeting all state requirements for carrying a firearm on duty.

Armed security is typically recommended for high-risk locations including financial institutions, cannabis operations, jewelry stores, executive protection, and any environment where the threat level warrants an elevated response capability. Our armed officers are trained not only in firearm safety and use of force, but in threat de-escalation — ensuring that firearms are a last resort, not a first response.

We develop site-specific post orders and threat assessments for every armed security assignment. Our officers understand your facility, your risks, and your priorities — and they are fully supported by ISG's 24/7 dispatch and field supervision teams at all times.
